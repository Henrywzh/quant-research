# Gold, Silver, Copper, Oil, And Bitcoin

## Purpose

Track cross-asset price behavior across precious metals, industrial metals,
energy, and crypto as a regime and inflation-sensitivity input.

## Yahoo Finance Universe

- `GC=F` - gold futures
- `SI=F` - silver futures
- `HG=F` - copper futures
- `CL=F` - WTI crude oil futures
- `BTC-USD` - bitcoin spot

## Research Questions

- When do precious metals confirm macro stress or inflation fear?
- Does copper lead or lag broad cyclicals and industrial risk appetite?
- When does bitcoin behave like speculative beta versus macro hedge?

## Notes

- Futures-based Yahoo symbols are acceptable for directional tracking, but the
  research layer should remember they are not ETF total-return proxies.
- Relative spreads such as `gold / copper` or `silver / gold` may become useful
  regime indicators later.
