# Archived Ideas

Move ideas here when they are invalidated, superseded, or no longer worth
tracking. Preserve the original thesis and note why the idea was archived.

## Idea: IDEA-YYYY-MM-DD-ARCHIVE

- Idea ID: `IDEA-YYYY-MM-DD-ARCHIVE`
- Status: `archived`
- Source: `user|agent`
- Asset: `TICKER`
- Direction: `long|short`
- Time Horizon: `intraday|swing|position`
- Theme: `leadership|mean_reversion|macro|event|other`

### Thesis

Preserve the original thesis.

### Archive Reason

State whether the idea was invalidated, replaced, or simply expired.
