# Closed Trades

## Trade: TRD-YYYY-MM-DD-CLOSED

- Trade ID: `TRD-YYYY-MM-DD-CLOSED`
- Linked Idea: `IDEA-YYYY-MM-DD-01`
- Status: `closed`
- Asset: `TICKER`
- Direction: `long|short`
- Entry Date: `YYYY-MM-DD`
- Entry Price: `0.00`
- Size: `units or portfolio percentage`
- Stop: `price or condition`
- Target: `price, range, or scenario`
- Max Risk Budget: `capital or portfolio percentage`

### Original Plan

Preserve the original plan from the open trade.

### Updates

Summarize important trade management decisions.

### Exit

Record exit date, exit price, and exit reason.

### PnL

Record realized PnL and, if useful, R-multiple or percentage return.

### Decision Quality Review

Assess the quality of the idea, risk framing, and execution.

### Outcome Review

Separate what was skill, what was luck, and what was noise.

### Systematization Note

State whether this trade should move into the discretionary
systematization funnel.
