# Sector And Industry Relative Performance

## Purpose

Track major US sector ETFs plus semiconductor and software industry proxies to
study leadership, lagging behavior, and rotation across regimes.

## Yahoo Finance Universe

- `XLC` - communication services
- `XLY` - consumer discretionary
- `XLP` - consumer staples
- `XLE` - energy
- `XLF` - financials
- `XLV` - health care
- `XLI` - industrials
- `XLB` - materials
- `XLRE` - real estate
- `XLK` - technology
- `XLU` - utilities
- `SMH` - semiconductors
- `IGV` - software

## Research Questions

- Which sectors lead during risk-on versus defensive phases?
- Do semiconductors and software lead broad technology or break down earlier?
- Can sector-relative strength help validate broader market-regime calls?

## Notes

- Start with trailing relative returns over 1M, 3M, 6M, and 12M windows.
- Compare high-beta growth clusters (`XLK`, `SMH`, `IGV`, `XLY`) with defensives
  (`XLP`, `XLU`, `XLV`) to identify regime tension.
