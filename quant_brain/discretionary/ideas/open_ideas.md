# Open Ideas

## Idea: IDEA-YYYY-MM-DD-01

- Idea ID: `IDEA-YYYY-MM-DD-01`
- Status: `open`
- Source: `user|agent`
- Asset: `TICKER`
- Direction: `long|short`
- Time Horizon: `intraday|swing|position`
- Theme: `leadership|mean_reversion|macro|event|other`

### Thesis

Write the core discretionary thesis in one or two clear paragraphs.

### Why Now

Describe what changed, what is lining up, or why the timing matters now.

### Entry Trigger

List the condition that should be true before acting.

### Invalidation

State the level, condition, or scenario that breaks the thesis.

### Risk

Describe sizing logic, stop logic, and what the maximum acceptable loss is.

### What Would Prove This Wrong

List the evidence that would show the idea was poorly framed or mistimed.
