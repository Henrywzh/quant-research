# Invalidation Rules

This file defines how discretionary trades should express what would prove the
idea wrong.

## Principles

- Invalidation should be tied to the thesis, not only to pain tolerance.
- A price level alone is often not enough; include the broken condition when
  possible.
- Time-based invalidation is valid when the catalyst window matters.
- Macro or cross-asset invalidation should be stated when the trade depends on
  a regime assumption.
