# Open Trades

## Trade: TRD-YYYY-MM-DD-01

- Trade ID: `TRD-YYYY-MM-DD-01`
- Linked Idea: `IDEA-YYYY-MM-DD-01`
- Status: `open`
- Asset: `TICKER`
- Direction: `long|short`
- Entry Date: `YYYY-MM-DD`
- Entry Price: `0.00`
- Size: `units or portfolio percentage`
- Stop: `price or condition`
- Target: `price, range, or scenario`
- Max Risk Budget: `capital or portfolio percentage`

### Original Plan

Summarize the thesis, trigger, and expected path of the trade.

### Updates

Append dated updates as the trade evolves. Do not erase earlier updates.

### Exit

Leave blank until the trade is closed.

### PnL

Leave blank until the trade is closed.

### Decision Quality Review

Briefly assess whether the trade still matches the original plan.

### Outcome Review

Reserve for closure or major thesis changes.

### Systematization Note

State whether the setup looks repeatable enough to study quantitatively.
